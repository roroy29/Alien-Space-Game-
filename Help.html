The Gameplay

  • The aim of the game is to get as many points as possible by moving your alien and dogging evil green aliens in the pink UFO.

  • You will get points if the evil aliens touch the bottom red bar.

  • If you die the game will show your score and you can play again

Controls

  • W - Moving UP
  • A - Moving LEFT
  • S - Moving DOWN
  • D - Moving RIGHT
